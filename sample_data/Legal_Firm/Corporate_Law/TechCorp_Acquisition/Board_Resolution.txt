BOARD RESOLUTION

RESOLVED, that the Board of Directors of TechCorp Industries hereby:

1. APPROVES the proposed acquisition transaction with an aggregate 
   value of $45,000,000;

2. AUTHORIZES the Chief Executive Officer and Chief Financial Officer
   to negotiate and execute all documents necessary to consummate 
   the transaction;

3. AUTHORIZES the engagement of Morrison & Blackwell LLP as legal 
   counsel for the transaction;

4. DIRECTS management to provide updates at each regular Board meeting
   until closing.

ADOPTED by unanimous written consent on February 15, 2025.

Corporate Secretary
