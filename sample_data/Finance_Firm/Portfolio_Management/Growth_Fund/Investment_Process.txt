GROWTH FUND INVESTMENT PROCESS

PHILOSOPHY:
We seek companies with sustainable competitive advantages,
strong management teams, and long runways for growth.

SCREENING CRITERIA:
1. Revenue growth > 15% annually
2. Expanding margins
3. High return on invested capital (>15%)
4. Strong balance sheet
5. Large addressable market

PORTFOLIO CONSTRUCTION:
- 30-50 holdings
- Maximum position: 10%
- Sector limits: 50%
- Market cap > $10B

SELL DISCIPLINE:
- Thesis broken
- Valuation excessive (>50x forward PE)
- Better opportunity identified
- Position size > 10%

RISK MANAGEMENT:
- Daily P&L monitoring
- Weekly factor exposure review
- Monthly drawdown analysis
